Wake On Lan

Wake on What ??

Wake on LAN is an Ethernet computer networking standard. It allows to turn on a computer with a "magic packet" send on the network, with protocol UDP on port 7 or 9.

It is a broadcast message, that is to say that it is send to all the computers of a local network.

But it will not wake up all of them !

The packet :

In fact, the magic packet contains the MAC address of the computer that you want to turn on. The others will see the packet, but reject it.

The packet contains first 48 bits at level '1' (that is equivalent to 6 bytes at 255, or in hexadecimal FF FF FF FF FF FF).

These bits are followed by sixteen times the mac address (that is also 48 bits long).

For example, if your MAC address is 01:23:45:67:89:ef, the magic packet that will turn on your computer is :
