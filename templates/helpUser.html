User Manual :

Registration :

Users cannot register to On/Off application. You need to ask the administrator of On/Off to create an account for you.

Please, when you are registred, change your password as soon as possible, and choose a strong one.

Connection :

Go to the application's main page. If you cannot see the window login, it means that your web browser does not support javascript and jquery. Please, download and use a recent version of Firefox or Chromium browser.

Functionalities of users' web interface :

With On/Off, you can see the status, power on or turn off :

  • all the hosts linked to you by the admin
  • all the hosts in farms you are member of
  • all the groups with access level lower than access level of a group you are member of
To join a group, a farm or to be associated to an host, contact your admin !

Other functionalities :

On/Off allows the admin to schedule tasks : wake on or turn off computers every day, every week, or every month or with custom time and date parameters.
